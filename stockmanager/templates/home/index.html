About wave:


Named after the Elliott wave principle for market cycle analysis, wave aims to assist you with keeping an eye on your portfolio and the stock market. You can create your own portfolio for trading and a watchlist to keep track of the rest. With our alert system you can receive notifications when your securities reach a certain limit. wave offers a user friendly design that is aimed to be helpful instead of confusing to the begining investor.
